Went in to wake Gus up from a 3-hour nap after spending the first half of the day at Magic Kingdom.

Me: You ready to go back and ride some more rides?

Gus (half-whining): I’m dooone.

Me: You’re done what?

Gus: Diiisney.

(Five minutes later he bounded out of the room, ready for more.)
